Helping To Transform Lives

Together We Can Make A Difference

Come together started in 28th August 2021, as we approached lifting of Covid-19 government guidelines, we felt it was an ideal time to provide a floating support service. After spending many years struggling with addiction problems, homelessness and poor mental health. I found it difficult to access services, the difficulty was not down to the services it was due to my own issues. After finally getting the help I needed in 2012 I spent the best part of my own recovery studying and researching services and issues that I faced. As we approached covid restrictions being lifted it became apparent that the need for a support service was greater than ever! - so myself, my partner and some friends decided that we was going to act!

We acquired 2 venues and created a voluntary organisation where people can come together to support the homeless, those who have drug and alcohol issues and those suffering from mental ill health. This is just the very start and we aim to reach as many people as possible and provide as much support as required. We aim to walk hand in hand with those individuals who need support and ensure they know they are not alone. The aim of our community group is to come together and strive towards happier and healthier Communities for all.

- Mat

About Us

We're a startup voluntary organisation based in St Helens. Our mission is to provide a safe space for people who are looking to improve their lives. By providing positive community activities, outreach programmes and more.

Local Care

Part of our ethos is to keep a positive outlook on life! Some say that charity start's at home, we do love that phrase, although we are not completely bound to it - we have pretty big arms here at the Come Together Hub and will reach out to anybody, anywhere, that needs our help! Everyone is welcome we can not say it enough - you dont have to struggle alone and we are always here to help if you need it.

As part of the steps to making the future better for all, our volunteers are committed to not just helping the vulnerable of the community, with handing out donation and offering advise and friendship, we also are incredibly proactive in trying to improve areas of neglect, providing the community with hands reaching out if they need it. This is not an easy task and we can not do it without your help. So please get involved by donating much needed living essentials such as toiletries, dry food, old blankets, clothes, tents or a small cash donation. It doesn't matter about the size of your donation because everything is needed, so lets Come Together and make a better future for all.

We are a small organisation and are always recruiting for Volunteers as well as accepting donations too. Here is where you can message your interest in donating, recruitment or enquiring over anything.
